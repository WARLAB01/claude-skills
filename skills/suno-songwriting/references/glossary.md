# Music Glossary for Suno Style Prompts

Use these terms in the Style Prompt field to guide Suno's generation.

## Tempo & Rhythm

**Tempo terms** (use in style prompt):
- Slow / Ballad / Adagio (66-76 BPM)
- Moderate / Andante (76-108 BPM)
- Upbeat / Allegro (120-168 BPM)
- Fast / Driving / Presto (168-200 BPM)

**Rhythm descriptors**: syncopated, swung, straight, shuffling, groovy, bouncy, plodding, driving, polyrhythmic, laid-back, punchy

## Dynamics & Energy

**Volume/intensity**: quiet, soft, intimate, hushed, building, swelling, crescendo, loud, powerful, explosive, anthemic, diminishing, fading, winding down

**Energy arc descriptors**: starts soft and builds, high energy throughout, dynamic range from whisper to roar, steady groove never lets up

**Positive**: uplifting, joyful, triumphant, hopeful, celebratory, playful, fun, carefree, energetic, empowering

**Negative**: melancholy, sad, mournful, dark, brooding, angry, aggressive, haunting, eerie

**Neutral/Complex**: nostalgic, bittersweet, wistful, dreamy, mysterious, intense, dramatic, epic, cinematic

## Style Prompt Formula

`Genre + Vocal style + Key instruments + Mood/energy + Production quality`

### Examples

**Rockabilly**: "Upbeat rockabilly with sassy female vocals, twangy guitar, standup bass, playful and defiant energy, vintage 50s production"

**Stadium Pop**: "Anthemic stadium pop, soaring male vocals, big drums, synth hooks, building to explosive chorus, polished modern production"

**Acoustic Ballad**: "Intimate acoustic ballad, fingerpicked guitar, soft male vocals, sparse arrangement, emotional and vulnerable, lo-fi warmth"

**Rock/Pop Hybrid**: "Driving rock-pop with punchy drums, distorted guitar riffs, catchy hooks, confident male vocals, arena-ready energy"

**Comedic/Novelty**: "Upbeat pop-rock with playful male vocals, tongue-in-cheek delivery, bouncy rhythm, catchy chorus, fun and irreverent"
